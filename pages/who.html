Our Purpose

Established in 2013, Airvox is a young, independent, IT consultancy based in the UK's South West, with quick access to Bristol, Bath, Taunton and surrounding areas.

Airvox grew out of the belief that organisations can and should be in control of their own technology direction, and that by doing so they could get more out of their IT investments.

Our mission is to make the best of technology available to everyone, with a focus on education, small to medium business and charitable institutions
